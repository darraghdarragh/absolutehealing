Acupuncture & Traditional Chinese Medicine (T.C.M.)

Acupuncture and T.C.M. date back thousands of years and aim to restore balance, relieve pain and support the body's natural healing.

Acupuncture: extremely fine sterile needles are placed at specific points to rebalance Qi.

Moxibustion: gently heated Artemisia (moxa) warms points to stimulate circulation.

Fire Cupping: cups create suction to mobilise blood/lymph, ease tension and recovery.

Tuina: Chinese medical massage harmonising organs, clearing channels, freeing Qi.

Herbal / Lifestyle: tailored formulas, diet and movement (Qi Gong / Tai Qi) suggested.

Indications: headaches, anxiety, insomnia, digestion, menstrual imbalance, pain.
